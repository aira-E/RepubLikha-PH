About

RepubLikha PH

A commission-based graphical art center in the Philippines. It’s name is a combination of Likha, a Filipino term for work of art. And Republika that represents the Philippines. We aim to deliver graphical arts that is not just aesthetically crafted, but is also budget-friendly, and accurate.

Terms and Conditions

Last Revised: January 2023

Mabuhay ang Republikha PH! This art center provides a service and products to our clients and may be used for in informational and promotional purposes only.. Because the Terms and Conditions contain legal obligations, please read them carefully.

Artist’s Reservations:

  1. Artist may decline a project
  2. Artists have the right to put a signature/watermark on the project except for logo commissions.
  3. Artists may use the artwork for a portfolio, website, and for other marketing purposes.
  4. Artists must state a minimum price to the maximum price of the project.

Client’s Reservations:

  1. Client may note their requests before the progress.
  2. Client may ask for minor changes.
  3. Client must state how much is his budget for the commission.
  4. Client can choose between paying half or full before the start of the commission.
  5. Client may request the artist to post the artwork at a certain date but cannot ask to not post the artwork or use it for any promotional matters at all.

Rendering Reservations:

  1. Client’s expectation for the quality is also depending on the:
    1. Client’s reference (Client’s Reference must be precise or clear)
    2. Time Rendering Allowance (Time allocation should be attainable)
  2. No cancellation of commission if the artwork is 50% progress or up.
  3. Artist will send sketches to the client. And will only start the commission if both parties agree to the arranged layout and price.
  4. Clients will receive 2 pictures. First, the fully rendered artwork. Second, the artwork without background.
  5. These are the grounds for the termination of the commission:
    1. If the client didn’t communicate for up to 30days.
